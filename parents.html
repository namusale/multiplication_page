To My Fellow Parent or Guardian



Thank for taking time to visit this webpage. Im of the view that your child's mastery of maths specifically multiplication tables is of importance to you.

This webpage covers the following:

  • Counting in numbers which begins in Year 1. A child counting in 2s is laying the foundation of mastery of multiplication table of 2. The page gives an opportunity to count in numbers from 1 to 12. The webpage also provides for learning and also practising by answering questions for each table.



  • Another aspect addressed in the pages is actual mastery of Times Table. To help child acquire thos skill, the following steps have been followed:

    • A list of Times Table facts have been provided in case the student needs some help with recall.


    • Provision to answer questions in each table has been given with every answer checked for correctness


    • After a child has mastered the tables,quick fire questions are given. These are timed at 6 seconds (like Year 4 Multiplication tabel test) and answers are check for correctness as usual.




This content is as a result of 6 years of following the learning journey of my children in primary school years. Im by no means a teacher but a parent,like many other parents, learning as their children learn. I hope you find this content useful

Thank you

Mum of 4 children in preschool to year 5
